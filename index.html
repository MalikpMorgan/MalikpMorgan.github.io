About me

My name is Malik Morgan and I've been working as a software engineer at a nonprofit called Organic Material Review Institute (OMRI) for the past 4 years. I've always been interested in building tech solutions to monitor, understand and improve the environment and OMRI seemed like a good place to start. I am looking to enter into a field that works with more robust data that pertains to the environment and conservation.

Recently I've been working on some projects to explore this area using different G.I.S. applications and frameworks like google maps api, leefmap, satellite image resources, and jupyter notebooks to analyze datasets that use geospatial data. I am still fairly new to this tech and recently decided to enroll in a course through OpenCV to learn more about AI ,machine learning, computer vision, and deep learning python libraries.
